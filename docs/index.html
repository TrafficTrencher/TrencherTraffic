Ticker: TRAFFIC

Today, a real pharmacy delivery route is streamed live — because it’s my job. I operate as an independent medical delivery driver where income remains directly tied to human time, attention, and miles driven.

Traffic Trencher documents what happens when that reality collides with automation.

The Shift Happening Now

Autonomous transportation is moving from research into commercial deployment. Companies such as Tesla, Uber, Amazon, Waymo and major logistics networks are actively building systems designed to operate without human drivers.

The transition will not occur through permission — it will occur when autonomous systems are technologically capable and economically more efficient than human drivers.

For centuries technological revolutions followed the same pattern:

  1. Industry adopts automation
  2. Workers are displaced
  3. Society reacts afterward

The Industrial Revolution mechanized labor.
The Internet automated information work.
Autonomous mobility targets driving itself.

Traffic Trencher reverses that order.

Instead of waiting to be replaced, this project documents a delivery driver adapting in real time — using today’s labor and visibility to acquire the autonomous systems replacing the job.

Traffic Trencher exists to adapt before that threshold is crossed.
