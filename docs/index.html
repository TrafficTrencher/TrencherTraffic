The Operation

How the grind becomes capital for autonomy.

Phase 1 — Human Driver Era
  • Stream the route when safe (proof + reps).
  • Log miles (public scoreboard).
  • Build audience + operators who understand the mission.
Phase 2 — Attention Era
  • Reach Top-25 attention on FOMO.
  • Convert attention into equipment + automation budget.
  • Standardize routes, SOPs, and margins.
Phase 3 — Model 3 w/ FSD
  • Start autonomy testing on the same loop.
  • Compare: human vs FSD time, safety, cost, throughput.
  • Shift attention from driving → operations + community.
Phase 4 — Cybercab
  • Pre-order + purchase when available.
  • Graduate from driver to fleet operator.
  • Document the full transition live.
