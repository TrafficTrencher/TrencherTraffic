The Origin

I work a real medical delivery job as an independent contractor.

The route is predictable. The miles are fixed. The work exists because humans are still required to drive.

But that condition is changing.

Autonomous vehicles are moving into real commercial use — and delivery driving is one of the first jobs positioned to change.

At some point, companies won’t need drivers like me. They’ll deploy autonomous vehicles instead.

So I made a decision:

Rather than waiting for automation to replace my job, I’m building toward replacing it myself.

Traffic Trencher documents that transition live — turning one contractor route into the foundation for an autonomous medical delivery operation.

Not escaping the system. Outrunning it.

The Plan

Streaming turns labor into visibility.

Visibility becomes capital for automation.

Creator fees are claimed transparently every 1,000 miles and reinvested into the path from human-driven delivery → autonomous delivery operations.
