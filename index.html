Ticker: TRAFFIC

Traffic Trencher is a live operational experiment documenting what happens when automation collides with real human work.

Each weekday, a real pharmacy delivery route is driven and streamed live using Starlink connectivity — turning ordinary labor into publicly verifiable proof-of-work.

The Shift Happening Now

Transportation is entering the autonomous era. Companies like Tesla, Waymo, Amazon, and major logistics networks are deploying systems designed to operate without human drivers.

This transition does not occur through permission — it occurs when autonomous systems become economically superior to human labor.

Why Stream the Route

Streaming converts invisible labor into visible data. Miles driven become measurable progress. Progress becomes capital.

The goal is not commentary — it is documentation.

The Mission

Creator revenue and checkpoint milestones are used to acquire an autonomous-capable vehicle.

From there:

  • Human driver
  • AI-supervised operator
  • Multi-route manager
  • Autonomous delivery fleet operator

The entire transition is documented live — from inside the driver’s seat.
