The Pattern (Industrial Revolution → Now)

For generations, big technological shifts followed the same order: corporations adopt automation first, workers get displaced second, and society reacts last.

Traffic Trencher reverses it: instead of waiting to be replaced, I’m using today’s labor + creator economy visibility to buy the autonomy that would replace me — and scale into the operator role before the big players force the transition.

What This Aligns With

Autonomous transportation is moving from testing into real-world commercial deployment. The companies building this shift won’t ask drivers for permission — they’ll replace the job when the tech is ready.
