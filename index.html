The Origin Lore

In 2016, Elon Musk was sitting in Los Angeles traffic and publicly vented that it was driving him insane. That frustration became The Boring Company.

Ten years later, I’m sitting in traffic on my daily medical courier route.

Different city. Same brake lights. Same wasted time.

The route is fixed. The miles are guaranteed. The time is already being spent.

So I made a decision: tokenize it.

Don’t escape traffic. Own it.

That’s where TRAFFIC TRENCHER was born.

I’m tokenizing my daily medical courier route in Southwest Florida on pump.fun — turning the grind into a live, community-driven experiment.
