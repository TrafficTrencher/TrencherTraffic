CCM (Courier Content Maxxing) • Proof of Miles • Creator Fees

Welcome to the real-world wage maxxing CCM (Courier Content Maxxing) meta.

I’m tokenizing my daily medical courier route in Southwest Florida on pump.fun — turning the grind into a live, community-driven experiment.

Every weekday, I’m behind the wheel streaming the loop live via Starlink Mini Roam Unlimited: clipping road rage moments, logging miles, documenting the hustle, and sharing my real-time Trade With FOMO journey on the FOMO app.

Creator fees from the token get claimed transparently every 1,000 miles — and redeployed straight into stream upgrades, route expenses, and rewards for holders.

The endgame? Earn enough through creator rewards and community support to buy my AI replacement: a Tesla with Full Self-Driving (FSD). Once I can go hands-off the wheel, I’ll be able to dive deeper into the FOMO trenches, stream more actively, and finally break free from the fixed medical route — exploring more of Florida and eventually taking the stream out of state.
