Thesis

The project in one sentence: I’m replacing my own job before automation replaces it — and documenting the transition live from inside pharmacy logistics.

The Master Thesis

Trencher Traffic is a real-world experiment documenting the transition from human-operated labor to autonomous systems in real time, beginning with independent pharmacy courier logistics. I stream a real medical route while autonomy matures — converting work into documented proof-of-grind, then reinvesting into autonomy adoption so labor can detach from human effort and reconnect to ownership.

Calm rule: document what’s true today, never promise what isn’t.

The Three Non-Negotiables
  • Reality must always be true. No staged progress. No inflated claims.
  • Safety over content. Driving attention is non-negotiable.
  • Mission outlives markets. The experiment continues regardless of price or views.
